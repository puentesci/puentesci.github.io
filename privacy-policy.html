Privacy Policy

How we handle information collected through Puentescientific.com

Last updated: September 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily share with us through the contact form on Puentescientific.com, including your name, email address, company name, and message.

We also collect basic, non-personally identifiable details about your visit—such as browser type and pages viewed—to help us understand how visitors use the Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the contact form is used only to respond to your inquiries and communicate with you. Automatically collected information is used for internal analytical purposes to improve the Site.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share information with trusted third parties that assist us in operating our website, provided those parties agree to keep the information confidential.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your personal information when it is submitted through our contact form. However, transmission over the internet is not completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

Puentescientific.com may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
